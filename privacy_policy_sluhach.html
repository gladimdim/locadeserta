Privacy Policy

Dmytro Gladkyi built the "Sluha.ch" application.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding my policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use the app.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not collect any user, device or any other data from players. The app does not require internet connection to work. Save files are saved to disk of device and are not uploaded in any way to any other server. No metrics are saved, except those related to the gameplay in-game statistics. When you uninstall the game all the data is removed and cannot be retrieved.
